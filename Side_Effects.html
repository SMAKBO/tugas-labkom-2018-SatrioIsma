Side effects can be:

Mental Disoders

Haemolysis, hemoglobinuria and acute renal failure. Local irritations such as discomfort and burning of the rectum. Potentially Fatal: Cardiac arrhythmias; overload circulation, pulmonary edema and CHF.

Not everyone has the following side effects. There may be some side effects not mentioned above. If you have concerns about certain side effects, consult your doctor or pharmacist.
